Farris Heritage Trust

A Private Inter-Generational Trust

The Farris Heritage Trust preserves family capital across generations while supporting education, productive work, and responsible citizenship.

Establishment Purpose Beneficiaries Governance Benefits Applications

Establishment of the Trust

The Farris Heritage Trust was established to preserve family capital and provide structured support for future generations.

The Trust is administered under the laws of the State of Texas by fiduciaries who are legally obligated to act in the best interests of the Trust and its beneficiaries.

Purpose of the Trust

The Trust was created to promote long-term family stability while encouraging independence, achievement, and responsible stewardship.

  • Education and intellectual development
  • Personal responsibility and productive work
  • Ethical conduct and civic responsibility
  • Preservation of family capital for future generations

Trust benefits are intended to support self-reliance rather than dependency.

Beneficiaries

  • Direct descendants of the Grantor unless specifically excluded by the Trust instrument
  • Certain individuals designated in the Trust instrument
  • Certain educational institutions and charitable organizations designated in the Trust instrument

Eligibility for benefits may require verification of identity or lineage according to the administrative procedures established by the Trust.

Trust Governance

Grantor

Ronald Lafe Farris established the Trust and defined its guiding principles, purposes, and governing structure.

Trustee

The Trustee manages Trust assets, reviews claims, maintains records, files required returns, and administers the Trust according to its governing instrument.

Trust Protector

The Protector provides independent oversight and helps ensure that administration remains faithful to the Grantor’s intent.

Benefits and Programs

Click a benefit below for a PDF summary describing the program, general eligibility, and claim requirements.

Educational Assistance

Reimbursement of qualified tuition and required fees for undergraduate, graduate, vocational, and certification programs.

Basic Support Assistance

Limited income support for eligible beneficiaries experiencing financial hardship under Trust standards.

Entrepreneurial Support

Limited start-up assistance for small businesses, vocational development, and productive career advancement.

Heritage Recognition Program

A one-time recognition benefit for descendants who preserve qualifying family names.
